Frank Jansen van Rensburg describes herself as a "gonzo stylist" and an "amateur loop digger". She is largely inspired by the connective tissue that binds creative projects together, and so her work is often collaborative and heavily emotive. Through fashion, Frank aims to convey truthful narrative by inserting herself in the work. She acheives this largely through styling, visual merchandising, designing and creative direction.
